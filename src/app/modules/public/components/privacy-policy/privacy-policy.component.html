3. Artificial Intelligence Processing

3.1 AI Sub-Processors

The Platform's AI features are powered by third-party AI providers. When you submit queries or upload documents to LegiSearch, LegiDraft, or LegiLyze, your Content is transmitted to and processed by the following sub-processors:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS Bedrock) — Powers core AI features using Claude language models hosted within AWS infrastructure. AI processing is covered under our AWS Business Associate Agreement (BAA). Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit, stays within US regions, and is never used to train AI models.
  • OpenAI, LLC — Used for document embedding and semantic search features. OpenAI's privacy policy governs their processing.

3.2 AI Conversation and Research History

Your AI queries, responses, uploaded documents, and research history are stored in our database to provide conversation history, bookmarking, and continuity features. You can delete individual conversations or analyses at any time from within the Platform.

3.3 Sensitive Information

While AI processing is covered under our AWS Business Associate Agreement, you remain responsible for ensuring that any information you submit to the Platform complies with your professional confidentiality obligations, including HIPAA, state privacy laws, and the Rules of Professional Conduct. Exercise professional judgment when submitting sensitive client data to AI features.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Platform and its AI features
  • Process AI queries and return AI Output through our third-party AI providers
  • Maintain your account, organization, and subscription

  • Process billing and payments

We do not use your Content or AI queries to train our own AI models or sell your data to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

We use Amazon Web Services (AWS) for cloud infrastructure and data storage. Our infrastructure is covered under the AWS Business Associate Addendum for HIPAA-eligible services. Other service providers (payment processors, monitoring tools) are bound by appropriate data processing agreements.

5.3 Legal Requirements

5.5 No Sale of Data

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information, client data, or case information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your information:

  • TLS encryption for all data in transit between your browser and our servers
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive fields (personal identifying information, financial data)
  • Role-based access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel
  • Multi-tenant data isolation ensuring your organization's data is segregated from other organizations
  • BCrypt password hashing with high work factors
  • Audit logging of all access to sensitive data and administrative actions
  • AWS infrastructure with HIPAA-eligible security controls

No system is completely immune from security breaches. In the event of a breach that affects your data, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

Upon termination or account deletion, we will delete your data within 90 days, subject to legal holds, dispute resolution requirements, and applicable law.
