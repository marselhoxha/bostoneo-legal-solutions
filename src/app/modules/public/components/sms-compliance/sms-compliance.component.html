1. Opt-In Flow & Consent Collection

How Clients Opt-In to SMS Messages
1
Client Invitation

Client is invited by their law firm to complete intake on the Bostoneo Solutions platform.

2
Navigate to Intake Form

Client navigates to the intake form URL:

/public/intake-forms
3
Complete Required Information

Client provides contact information including name, email, and mobile phone number.

4
SMS Consent Checkbox (OPTIONAL)

Client is presented with an UNCHECKED, optional SMS consent checkbox with full disclosure language.

Important: The checkbox is NEVER pre-checked. Clients must actively select it to consent.
5
Consent Recorded

Upon form submission, consent status and timestamp are recorded in our database for compliance purposes.

4. Privacy Policy - SMS Section

Our Privacy Policy includes a dedicated SMS/Text Messaging section. View the full policy at /public/privacy-policy

SMS/Text Messaging Privacy (Section 4)
Mobile Information Collection

When you provide your mobile phone number and consent to receive SMS text messages from Bostoneo Solutions, we collect and use your mobile phone number solely for the purpose of communicating with you regarding your legal matter.

Mobile Information Protection

We will NEVER sell, rent, share, or disclose your mobile phone number or any information collected through our SMS messaging program to any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Your mobile information is used exclusively for direct communication between you and Bostoneo Solutions regarding your legal representation.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Consent Not Required

Your consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of any service we provide.

View Full Privacy Policy

5. Opt-Out Mechanism

Clients can opt out of SMS messages at any time using any of the following methods:

Reply STOP

Reply "STOP" to any message received. Alternative keywords: CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, QUIT, END

Contact Support

Email support@bostoneosolutions.com to request removal from SMS communications.

Opt-Out Confirmation Message:

"Bostoneo Solutions: You have been unsubscribed and will no longer receive SMS messages from us. Reply HELP for assistance."

6. HELP Response

When a client replies HELP to any message, they receive the following response:

"Bostoneo Solutions: For assistance, contact your law firm directly or email support@bostoneosolutions.com. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Msg&Data rates may apply."
