General Overview
This app is designed to manage users, clients, and invoices efficiently while maintaining high security. It includes features such as user authentication, client management, invoice handling, and activity tracking.
The app handles user profiles, client information, and invoices. It tracks user activities like logins and account updates, while ensuring data security through token-based authentication and role-based access control.
The app uses JWT token-based authentication, role and permission-based access, two-factor authentication (2FA), and brute-force attack prevention measures, ensuring high levels of security.
User Account Management
To create an account, provide a unique email address. You’ll receive an email to verify your account. After verification, you can log in and manage your profile.
If you forget your password, request a password reset via your email. You’ll receive a link to reset your password, which is valid for 24 hours.
2FA adds an extra layer of security by sending a verification code to your phone after you enter your password. You must input this code to complete the login process.
Security & Privacy
User activities such as logins, profile changes, and suspicious behavior are logged with details like IP address, device, browser, and time. This ensures transparency and security.
After six failed login attempts, your account will be temporarily locked to protect against brute-force attacks. You will receive a notification, and your account will unlock after a specified time or with assistance from support.
The app uses encrypted tokens, role-based access control, and activity logging to ensure your data is secure from unauthorized access or tampering.
Client Management
You can add and update client details like name, address, and status. Clients can be individuals or institutions, and you can associate them with specific invoices.
Yes, clients can be searched by name and filtered by status. The app supports pagination to manage large datasets and ensure easy access to client data.
You can export client information in spreadsheet formats (CSV, XLSX) for backups, reporting, or analysis.
Invoice Management
You can create, edit, and manage invoices. Each invoice is linked to a specific client, and you can track payment statuses or print the invoices for mailing.
Yes, invoices can be exported in spreadsheet formats (CSV, XLSX) or as PDF documents. This feature enables easy archiving or printing of invoices for record-keeping.
Yes, you can track whether an invoice is paid, pending, or overdue. Invoice statuses help you stay on top of payments and manage client accounts efficiently.
Reports & Exports
The app generates reports for clients and invoices. You can export client lists and invoices in spreadsheet formats for further analysis or share them with other departments.
Yes, you can export client data and invoices to CSV or XLSX files for backups or external processing. This makes it easier to maintain and share critical data.
