Case Valuation & Damage Calculator

This is where Legience replaces your spreadsheets. The Valuation tab gives you a comprehensive damage calculator that breaks down every element of your case value — and then uses AI to analyze comparable cases, score your case strength, and estimate a settlement range.

You start by adding individual damage elements. Legience supports 8 damage categories: past medical expenses, future medical expenses, lost wages, loss of earning capacity, household services, pain & suffering, mileage (using the current IRS rate), and other damages. For each element, you specify the amount, calculation method, and confidence level.

When you click "Calculate & Analyze," the AI does three things: it calculates your economic and non-economic damages with a multiplier, it scores your case strength on a 1–10 scale, and it generates a settlement range (Low, Likely, High) with a visual bar chart. If the medical expenses exceed 60% of the policy limit, it automatically flags the case as underinsured with strategy recommendations.

  • 4 calculation methods per element — Actual amount, Formula-based (multiplier), Scheduled (standard rates), Expert opinion
  • Confidence levels — mark each element as High, Medium, or Low confidence to weight the analysis
  • Sync from medical records — one click pulls all medical billing data into the calculator, no re-entering
  • Comparable case analysis — AI analyzes similar cases by injury type and jurisdiction
  • Comparative negligence — apply a percentage reduction and see the adjusted total instantly
